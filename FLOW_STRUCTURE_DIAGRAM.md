## Color Scheme

- **Success Green**: `#126930` (Winner status)

- **Text Secondary**: `#506179` (Body text)
- **Border**: `#edeef1` (Card borders)
- **Background**: `#eceff2` (Flow frame)
- **Surface**: `#ffffff` (Card backgrounds)

- **Variant B**: `#0eab43` (Green)
- **Variant C**: `#f59e42` (Orange)
